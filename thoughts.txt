Key Features to Include:

Product Catalog:
    Product listing and detail pages.
    Categories and filters (by price, category, etc.).
    Search functionality.

User Authentication:
    Registration, login, and logout.
    User profiles and dashboards.

Shopping Cart:
    Add, remove, and update items in the cart.
    Cart preview and checkout flow.

Order Management:
    Order creation, status tracking.
    Invoice generation.

Payment Integration:
    Integrate a payment gateway (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).

Admin Dashboard:
    Manage products, categories, orders, and users.

Responsive Design:
Ensure the site looks good on both desktop and mobile devices.
